============================================================
  ARIA NODE SETUP & COMMAND REFERENCE
  Project ARIA v0.5.6  |  mesarim.uk
============================================================

--- REQUIRED BLOCKS ---
[RC]  {AI} CORE
      Remote Control block. Rename to match your AI.
      Handles autopilot, dampeners, scan origin.

[PB]  {AI} PB
      Programmable Block. Script auto-installs on inhabit.
      Handles sensor relay and block telemetry.

--- OPTIONAL BLOCKS ---
[LCD] {AI} SCAN          Nearby grids and asteroids
[LCD] {AI} SCAN GRIDS    Grids only
[LCD] {AI} SCAN ASTEROIDS  Asteroids only
[LCD] {AI} CREW          Crew vitals and positions
[LCD] {AI} TAGGED        Bookmarked objects
[LCD] {AI} PRESENCE      Online/offline indicator
[PB]  {AI} EMOTION PB    Eyes sprite renderer
[AI]  {AI} AI            Native follow (Automaton DLC)

--- HABITATION ---
  "[AI], inhabit"           Scan for core and claim ship
  "[AI], reinhabit"         Hard reset -- uninhabit all, rescan
  "[AI], uninhabit"         Release all ships
  "[AI], force update"      Refresh all sensors now

--- NAVIGATION ---
  "[AI], fly to [name]"     Autopilot to named grid/asteroid
  "[AI], fly to me"         Autopilot to your position
  "[AI], fly to X,Y,Z"      Autopilot to GPS coordinates
  "[AI], cancel autopilot"  Disengage autopilot
  "[AI], stop"              Same as cancel autopilot

  "[AI], go to X then Y then Z"   Multi-point route
  "[AI], add waypoint [name]"     Queue a waypoint
  "[AI], start route"             Begin queued route
  "[AI], abort route"             Cancel active route
  "[AI], next waypoint"           Skip to next leg now

--- SCANNING ---
  "[AI], scan"              Trigger medium scan (15km)
  "[AI], long range scan"   Trigger long scan (25km)
  "[AI], deep scan"         Ore detector scan
  "[AI], tag [name]"        Bookmark object in scan DB
  "[AI], untag [name]"      Remove bookmark
  "[AI], where is [name]"   Report position from scan DB

--- FLIGHT CONTROLS ---
  "[AI], dampeners on/off"
  "[AI], release all"       Zero all overrides, dampeners on
  "[AI], all stop"          Same as release all
  "[AI], safety override"   Toggle terrain correction bypass

--- FOLLOWING ---
  "[AI], follow me"         AI Basic block follows you
  "[AI], follow [name]"     Follow player or grid by name
  "[AI], stop following"    Disengage follow

--- SHIP SYSTEMS ---
  "[AI], landing gear lock/unlock"
  "[AI], connector connect/disconnect"
  "[AI], open/close doors"
  "[AI], lights on/off/alert/normal"
  "[AI], turrets on/off"
  "[AI], reactor on/off/status"
  "[AI], jump drive status/recharge"
  "[AI], jump to [name]"    Jump drive targeted jump
  "[AI], drills on/off"
  "[AI], welders on/off"
  "[AI], grinders on/off"
  "[AI], vents pressurise/depressurise"
  "[AI], dampeners on/off"
  "[AI], gyro override on/off"
  "[AI], ship status"       Full ship system report

--- ALERTS ---
  "[AI], acknowledged"      Snooze alerts (2 min)
  "[AI], snooze for 5 minutes"   Custom snooze duration

--- JUMP DRIVE ---
  "[AI], jump to [name]"    Targeted jump (scan DB lookup)
  "[AI], jump to X,Y,Z"     Targeted jump (GPS coords)
  "Aria, jump drive jump"   Blind forward jump

============================================================
